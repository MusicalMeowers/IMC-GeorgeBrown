Check Your Understanding

You are Ace Auto Detail, specializing in automotive car care and detailing services for luxury European vehicles. During the bargain and problem-solving stage, the customer expresses concern over your premium price point. You know your price is, on average, 20% higher than the competition. You fear you may lose the sale. What is the best tactic to take with this customer?

Click the Correct Answer Below

Incorrect. Always try to meet a customer in the middle during the bargaining stage of negotiations. Empathize and tell the customer you understand their point of view while explaining one or two key unique selling features of your offering. This allows your customer to empathize with you and your higher price point. While this may not result in a sale, the customer will understand your value.

Incorrect. During the negotiation process, both parties should not reveal all their cards at once. If price is really the only barrier for a customer, you could look at a payment plan option, for which you could charge a small fee. This will give the appearance of affordability to the customer.

Great job! Reiterating your value proposition and unique selling features reminds customers of the benefits that they will receive by choosing your business.
